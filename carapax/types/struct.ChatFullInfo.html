Expand description

Contains full information about a chat.

Fields§

§id: i64

Unique identifier for this chat.

§chat_type: ChatFullInfoType

Type of the chat.

§accent_color: AccentColor

Identifier of the accent color for the chat name and backgrounds of the chat photo, reply header, and link preview.

§max_reaction_count: i64

The maximum number of reactions that can be set on a message in the chat.

§available_reactions: Option<Vec<ReactionType>>

List of available reactions allowed in the chat. If omitted, then all emoji reactions are allowed.

§background_custom_emoji_id: Option<String>

Custom emoji identifier of the emoji chosen by the chat for the reply header and link preview background.

§has_hidden_members: Option<bool>

Whether non-administrators can only get the list of bots and administrators in the chat.

§has_protected_content: Option<bool>

Whether messages from the chat can’t be forwarded to other chats.

§has_visible_history: Option<bool>

Whether new chat members will have access to old messages; available only to chat administrators.

§message_auto_delete_time: Option<i64>

The time after which all messages sent to the chat will be automatically deleted; in seconds.

§photo: Option<ChatPhoto>

Chat photo.

§pinned_message: Option<Message>

The most recent pinned message (by sending date).

§profile_accent_color: Option<ProfileAccentColor>

Identifier of the accent color for the chat’s profile background.

§profile_background_custom_emoji_id: Option<String>

Custom emoji identifier of the emoji chosen by the chat for its profile background.

§active_usernames: Option<Vec<String>>

The list of all active chat usernames; for private chats, supergroups and channels.

§username: Option<String>

Username, for private chats, supergroups and channels.

§description: Option<String>

Description, for supergroups, channels and group chats.

§invite_link: Option<String>

Primary invite link, for supergroups, channels and group chats.

§title: Option<String>

Title, for supergroups, channels and group chats.

§can_send_paid_media: Option<bool>

Whether paid media messages can be sent or forwarded to the channel chat.

The field is available only for channel chats.

§linked_chat_id: Option<i64>

Unique identifier for the linked chat, i.e. the discussion group identifier for a channel and vice versa; for supergroups and channel chats.

§bio: Option<String>

Bio of the other party in a private chat.

§birthdate: Option<Birthdate>

For private chats, the date of birth of the user.

§business_intro: Option<BusinessIntro>

For private chats with business accounts, the intro of the business.

§business_location: Option<BusinessLocation>

For private chats with business accounts, the location of the business.

§business_opening_hours: Option<BusinessOpeningHours>

For private chats with business accounts, the opening hours of the business.

§emoji_status_custom_emoji_id: Option<String>

Custom emoji identifier of the emoji status of the chat or the other party in a private chat.

§emoji_status_expiration_date: Option<i64>

Expiration date of the emoji status of the chat or the other party in a private chat, in Unix time, if any.

§first_name: Option<String>

First name of the other party in a private chat.

§has_private_forwards: Option<bool>

Whether privacy settings of the other party in the private chat allows to use tg://user?id=<user_id> links only in chats with the user.

§has_restricted_voice_and_video_messages: Option<bool>

Whether the privacy settings of the other party restrict sending voice and video note messages in the private chat.

§last_name: Option<String>

Last name of the other party in a private chat.

§personal_chat: Option<Chat>

For private chats, the personal channel of the user.

§can_set_sticker_set: Option<bool>

Whether the bot can change the group sticker set.

§custom_emoji_sticker_set_name: Option<String>

For supergroups, the name of the group’s custom emoji sticker set. Custom emoji from this set can be used by all users and bots in the group.

§has_aggressive_anti_spam_enabled: Option<bool>

Whether aggressive anti-spam checks are enabled in the supergroup. The field is only available to chat administrators.

§is_direct_messages: Option<bool>

Whether the chat is the direct messages chat of a channel.

§is_forum: Option<bool>

Whether the supergroup chat is a forum (has topics enabled).

§join_to_send_messages: Option<bool>

Whether users need to join the supergroup before they can send messages.

§join_by_request: Option<bool>

Whether all users directly joining the supergroup need to be approved by supergroup administrators.

§location: Option<ChatLocation>

For supergroups, the location to which the supergroup is connected.

§parent_chat: Option<Chat>

Information about the corresponding channel chat; for direct messages chats only.

§slow_mode_delay: Option<i64>

For supergroups, the minimum allowed delay between consecutive messages sent by each unprivileged user; in seconds.

§sticker_set_name: Option<String>

For supergroups, name of the group sticker set.

§unrestrict_boost_count: Option<i64>

For supergroups, the minimum number of boosts that a non-administrator user needs to add in order to ignore slow mode and chat permissions.

§permissions: Option<ChatPermissions>

Default chat member permissions, for groups and supergroups.

§accepted_gift_types: Option<AcceptedGiftTypes>

Information about types of gifts that are accepted by the chat or by the corresponding user for private chats.
